About Elf Magic

Welcome to Elf Magic, your go-to destination for making the holiday season sparkle with Elf on the Shelf adventures! Founded by Holly Evergreen, a mom of three with a passion for creating unforgettable Christmas memories, Elf Magic is all about bringing joy, creativity, and a touch of North Pole magic to families everywhere.

Our Mission

At Elf Magic, our mission is simple: to help parents craft magical holiday moments without the stress. We know how busy the holiday season can be, so we provide easy-to-implement Elf on the Shelf ideas, free printables, and tips to keep your kids enchanted from December 1st to Christmas Eve. Whether you're a seasoned elf enthusiast or new to the tradition, we're here to make every day a festive adventure!

Our Story

Elf Magic began over a decade ago when Holly struggled to come up with fresh elf ideas for her own children. After countless late-night brainstorming sessions and a few "oops" moments (like forgetting to move the elf!), she started sharing her ideas with friends and family. The response was overwhelming, and Elf Magic was born as a way to share creative, practical, and kid-approved elf scenarios with parents worldwide.

Meet the Team

Our small but dedicated team is made up of parents, crafters, and holiday enthusiasts who love spreading Christmas cheer. Led by Holly, we work year-round to dream up new elf antics, design printables, and test ideas to ensure they're fun, feasible, and full of magic. We're based in a cozy office filled with candy canes, glitter, and a whole lot of elf spirit!

Thank you for joining us on this festive journey. We can't wait to help you make this holiday season the most magical yet!
