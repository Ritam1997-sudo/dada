Privacy Policy

At Elf Magic, we value your privacy as much as a perfectly placed elf on Christmas Eve! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website, elfmagic.com. By using our site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to make your experience with Elf Magic as magical as possible. This includes:

  • Personal Information: When you fill out our contact form or comment on a blog post, we may collect your name, email address, and any other details you provide.
  • Usage Data: We use cookies and analytics tools to track how you interact with our site, such as pages visited and time spent.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, like your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us spread holiday cheer! We use it to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and comments.
  • Improve our website content and user experience.
  • Send you updates about new Elf on the Shelf ideas (only if you opt in).
  • Analyze site performance and traffic trends.

3. Sharing Your Information

We don't sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With service providers (e.g., analytics or hosting services) who help us operate the site, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Cookies

Like a plate of Christmas cookies, we use cookies to enhance your experience. These small files track your preferences and help us understand site usage. You can manage cookie settings in your browser. See our Cookie Policy for details.

5. Your Choices

You have control over your information:

  • Opt out of cookies via your browser settings.
  • Contact us at hello@elfmagic.com to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, like using secure servers and encryption. However, no online platform is 100% secure, so please use caution when sharing sensitive information.

7. Children's Privacy

Elf Magic is family-friendly, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have such data, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here, with the "Last Updated" date below. Please check back periodically.

9. Contact Us

Have questions about our Privacy Policy? Reach out to us at:
