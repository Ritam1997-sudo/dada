About Biswanath Chowdhury

Dedicated to Your Healing Journey

With over 15 years of experience in physiotherapy, Biswanath Chowdhury has helped thousands of patients recover from injuries, manage chronic pain, and improve their quality of life. His approach combines traditional techniques with modern research-based methods to provide comprehensive care tailored to each individual's needs.

Biswanath specializes in orthopedic rehabilitation, sports injuries, neurological conditions, and geriatric care. His gentle yet effective treatment techniques have earned him a reputation as one of the most trusted physiotherapists in the region.

Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy services designed to address a wide range of conditions and promote optimal physical well-being.

Orthopedic Rehabilitation

Specialized therapy for musculoskeletal injuries, joint replacements, and post-surgical rehabilitation to restore mobility and function.

  • Joint and muscle pain treatment
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Fracture rehabilitation

Sports Injury Management

Comprehensive treatment for athletes of all levels, focusing on injury prevention, rehabilitation, and performance enhancement.

  • Sports-specific rehabilitation
  • Injury prevention strategies
  • Performance enhancement

Neurological Rehabilitation

Specialized care for patients with neurological conditions to improve function, independence, and quality of life.

  • Stroke rehabilitation
  • Balance and coordination training
  • Functional mobility improvement

Chronic Pain Management

Multidisciplinary approach to manage and alleviate chronic pain conditions, improving daily function and quality of life.

  • Personalized pain reduction programs
  • Manual therapy techniques
  • Self-management strategies

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Specialized care for older adults addressing age-related conditions, mobility issues, and fall prevention.

  • Balance and fall prevention
  • Mobility enhancement
  • Arthritis management

Home Physiotherapy

Convenient, personalized care delivered in the comfort of your home, eliminating travel constraints for patients with mobility limitations.

  • Personalized home programs
  • Home safety assessments
  • Caregiver training
